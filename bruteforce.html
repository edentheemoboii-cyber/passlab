Brute Force Attack

What brute force means

A brute-force attack tries every possible combination until the correct password is found. It does not guess intelligently. It simply keeps trying.

aaa → aab → aac → aad → ... → abc FOUND

Experiment Design

  1. Use lowercase letters and digits only.
  2. Test short passwords only, such as 3 to 5 characters.
  3. Record attempts and time using Python.
  4. Compare how time changes when length increases.

Why length matters

With 26 lowercase letters and 10 digits, the program has 36 possible characters. Each additional password character multiplies the number of possible combinations by 36.

3 chars = 36 × 36 × 36
4 chars = 36 × 36 × 36 × 36
5 chars = 36 × 36 × 36 × 36 × 36
