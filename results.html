Results/Findings

Most Important Finding

Password length had a very strong effect on brute-force cracking time. Even when the character set stayed the same, adding one extra character greatly increased the number of combinations.

Most Dangerous Password

Common passwords were the easiest to crack because they appeared in the dictionary list. This shows why users should avoid simple words, names, and repeated patterns.
